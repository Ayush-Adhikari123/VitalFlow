Basic Health Package

Description

Test for General Health:

  • We provide a free ambulance service that is available 24/7 for your convenience and safety.
  • Home We offer free home services every 3 months. If additional services are required outside of this schedule, please contact us. Please note that charges may apply for services outside of the included packages.
  • We provide personalized diet plans. These are specifically tailored to meet individual health requirements. Our goal is to support your wellness journey through balanced nutrition.
  • Hemoglobin A1c: This test provides an average of blood sugar levels over the past 2-3 months, useful for managing diabetes.
  • To accommodate the elderly, we offer at-home sample collection and basic testing for free once every year (if needed), ensuring healthcare accessibility without the need for travel.
  • We offer liver and kidney function tests as part of our healthcare services.
  • Complete Blood Count (CBC): This test measures different components of the blood, including red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets.
  • Lipid Panel: This measures cholesterol levels, including total cholesterol, LDL (low-density lipoprotein), HDL (high-density lipoprotein), and triglycerides.
  • Blood Glucose Test: This measures blood sugar levels and is used to diagnose or monitor diabetes.
  • Liver Function Tests: These tests assess the health of the liver and include measurements such as ALT, AST, and bilirubin.
  • Kidney Function Tests: Tests like creatinine and blood urea nitrogen (BUN) help evaluate kidney function.
  • Thyroid Function Tests: These tests assess the thyroid gland's activity, including levels of TSH, T3, and T4 hormones.
